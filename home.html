Our Story

Bean Haven is a shop founded by a group of university friends who love coffee and are concerned about stray animal welfare. As students, we wanted to create something meaningful, combining our passion and sense of mission. We travel the world in search of unique coffee beans, bringing diverse flavors to Hong Kong's vibrant coffee culture. For every bag of coffee beans sold, we donate 5% of the proceeds directly to local stray cat rescue organizations, providing them with food, medical care, and shelter. We believe that a good cup of coffee should not only please your senses but also have a positive impact. From campus to the coffee cup in your hand, we brew change with every bag of coffee beans.
